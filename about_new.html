Our Story

Creating authentic experiences in Porto's creative heart

Welcome to Home2Home

Home2Home was born from a simple belief: travelers deserve more than just a place to sleep. They deserve a home that tells a story, connects them to local culture, and creates memories that last a lifetime.

Founded in the heart of Porto's vibrant Cedofeita district, we've carefully curated 14 unique apartments that blend contemporary comfort with authentic Portuguese character. Each space is more than accommodation‚Äîit's your gateway to experiencing Porto like a local.

Our team of local hosts and designers has poured passion into every detail, from selecting artisanal Portuguese ceramics to sourcing sustainable amenities. We believe that thoughtful hospitality can transform a simple stay into an unforgettable experience.

Our Design Philosophy

Every Home2Home apartment is a canvas where modern sophistication meets Portuguese soul.

Authentic Materials

We source locally-made ceramics, textiles, and artwork to create spaces that feel genuinely Portuguese while maintaining contemporary comfort.

Functional Beauty

Form follows function in every design choice. Beautiful spaces that work effortlessly for both short getaways and extended stays.

Sustainable Luxury

Luxury doesn't mean excess. We choose quality over quantity, supporting local artisans and sustainable practices.

"When we created Home2Home, we dreamed of spaces where travelers could experience Porto not as tourists, but as temporary locals. Every apartment tells a story of our city's rich culture, artistic heritage, and warm hospitality. We invite you to become part of that story."
