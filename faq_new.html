Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your Porto stay

Quick Answers

Find answers to the most common questions about staying with Home2Home

How do I book an apartment?

+

You can book directly through our website contact form, email us at hello@home2home.pt, or reach out via WhatsApp. We'll check availability and send you booking details with secure payment options.

What are your rates and do you offer discounts?

+

Our rates vary by season and apartment type. T0 studios start from €80/night, T1 apartments from €110/night, and T2 apartments from €150/night. We offer discounts for stays longer than 7 days and special rates for monthly bookings.

What's included in the price?

+

All our rates include high-speed Wi-Fi, weekly cleaning service, welcome essentials, fully equipped kitchen, premium bedding and towels, and 24/7 support. No hidden fees or tourist taxes.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for peak seasons (June-September) and holidays. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings when availability allows.

What time is check-in and check-out?

+

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. We offer flexible timing when possible - just let us know your travel schedule and we'll do our best to accommodate.

How does self check-in work?

+

We use secure digital lock systems. You'll receive detailed instructions and access codes 24 hours before arrival. Our local team is always available to assist if needed.

Can you accommodate early check-in or late check-out?

+

We try to accommodate early check-in and late check-out when possible, subject to availability. Early check-in may be available from 12:00 PM and late check-out until 2:00 PM at no extra charge.

Do you provide luggage storage?

+

Yes! If your apartment isn't ready for early arrival or you have a late departure, we can arrange secure luggage storage nearby. Just contact us in advance.

What kitchen facilities are provided?

+

All apartments have fully equipped kitchens with refrigerator, stovetop, oven, microwave, dishwasher, coffee machine, and all necessary cookware, dishes, and utensils. Perfect for preparing meals during your stay.

Is there air conditioning and heating?

+

Yes, all apartments have individual climate control with both heating and air conditioning to ensure your comfort year-round.

Do you provide cleaning service?

+

We include weekly cleaning service for all stays. For longer stays, we can arrange additional cleaning sessions. Fresh towels and linens are provided weekly.

Is there laundry facilities?

+

T1 and T2 apartments have washer/dryer combos. T0 studios have access to shared laundry facilities in the building. Detergent and fabric softener are provided.

Where exactly are you located?

+

We're located on Rua de Cedofeita in the heart of the Cedofeita district, Porto's creative and artistic neighborhood. You'll be walking distance from galleries, cafes, shops, and major attractions.

How do I get from the airport?

+

Take the Metro Purple Line from the airport to Trindade, then transfer to the Yellow Line to Bolhão station (about 20 minutes total). From Bolhão, it's a 5-minute walk to our apartments. Detailed directions will be provided.

Is parking available?

+

Street parking is available nearby (paid during weekdays 9 AM - 7 PM). We can also recommend secure paid parking garages within walking distance. Let us know if you're arriving by car.

What's within walking distance?

+

You're 5 minutes from Livraria Lello, 8 minutes from Crystal Palace Gardens, 2 minutes from Rua Miguel Bombarda's galleries and cafes, and 15 minutes from the historic Ribeira district.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in for full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a one-night charge. We understand plans change and try to be as flexible as possible.

Are pets allowed?

+

We welcome well-behaved pets in select apartments with advance notice and a small additional cleaning fee. Please contact us before booking to discuss your furry friend's needs.

Do you allow smoking?

+

All our apartments are strictly non-smoking. However, some apartments have balconies where smoking is permitted. Please respect this policy to maintain a pleasant environment for all guests.

Are there any age restrictions?

+

The primary guest must be at least 21 years old. We welcome families and children of all ages. Our apartments are designed to be comfortable and safe for guests of all ages.

Still Have Questions?

Our local team is here to help make your Porto stay perfect. Don't hesitate to reach out!
